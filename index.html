Frequently Asked Questions

Is the honey pure?

Yes, 100%! Our honey is completely pure, organic, and straight from our own bee colonies based in Taverekere, Bangalore. We undergo rigorous laboratory testing to verify that there are absolutely zero added sugars, corn syrups, chemicals, or colorings in our jars.

Does honey crystallize naturally?

Yes! Crystallization is a natural characteristic of real, unprocessed raw honey. It occurs when natural glucose separates from liquid state and forms solid crystals. In fact, if your honey *never* crystallizes, it might have been highly heated, ultra-filtered, or adulterated with sugar syrup. To return crystallised honey to liquid form, simply place the jar in a warm water bowl for a few minutes.

How should honey be stored?

Raw honey should be stored in a tightly sealed glass container at room temperature. Keep it in a dry kitchen cabinet away from direct sunlight. Do NOT refrigerate raw honey, as cold temperatures will speed up crystallization. Real raw honey has a very low moisture level and never truly spoils!

What makes your honey different?

Unlike industrial supermarket brands that flash-heat honey to 70°C and ultra-filter it to prevent crystallization (which kills valuable raw pollens and healthy enzymes), Kamadhenu Honey is completely unprocessed. We maintain our own hives, cold-extract honey, and bypass commercial blending completely, providing a direct farm-to-table honest honey experience.
